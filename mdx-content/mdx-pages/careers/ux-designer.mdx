**You are someone who**

-   Has meticulous and diligent attributes

-   Has a good understanding of end user requirements

-   Has the ability to act with professionalism and integrity

-   Have excellent people and managerial skills

-   Have excellent communication and interpersonal skills

-   Has the ability to organize projects well and efficiently plan
    milestones

**You will**

-   Design various mockups, wireframes, prototypes, and interfaces to
    make a user's life easier

-   Consult users and clients to understand their goals and needs

-   Enhance user experience by crafting a product or service that fits
    into the customer journey

-   Test user experiences and troubleshooting any UX problems

-   Build storyboards to easily conceptualize ideas to senior managers
    and various teams

-   Create research surveys to understand ease-of-use while working on
    various platforms

-   Ensure standardization is maintained with font, color, image, and
    style for consistency

-   Define various workflows, UI specifications, and interaction models

-   Assist with content development

-   Conduct competitor and customer analysis

**Basic qualifications**

-   Prior demonstrated experience of at least 3 to 5 years in UX
    experience for multiple devices

-   Strong portfolio of related projects

-   Experience with complex enterprise applications

-   Proficiency in Adobe Illustrator, Figma, Photoshop, UXPin, Invision,
    Sketch, Quartz, and Balsamiq

-   Understand and knowledge of UX standards

-   Work in cross-functional teams to understand product vision

-   Excellent IT and programming skills

**Preferred qualifications**

-   Degree in Interaction Design, Fine Arts, Human Computer Interaction,
    Computer Science, or other related fields

-   Passionate about following UX industry trends

-   Knowledge and expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript

-   Experience designing in international languages with complex product
    requirements

-   Ability to test usability and accessibility concern

-   Knowledge of various industry practices

-   Knowledge of Human Computer Interaction Element Design

-   A keen eye for detail
