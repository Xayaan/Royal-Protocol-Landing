**You are someone who**

- Can identify bottlenecks, and perform profiling, and load testing
- Is confident in your work and knows developers that are smart enough to use the tools you make
- Could code your way out of a job, sit back, sip coffee, and watch the metrics roll in while the servers scale seamlessly

**You will**

- Work with designers and product owners, and QA to evolve requirements into working applications
- Research, recommend, and implement solutions to challenging business requirements
- Collaborate with the engineering team to standardize tools and processes
- Develop and maintain solutions to meet SLAs for highly performant, highly available, and fault-tolerant distributed systems
- Lead efforts to foster a culture of Cloud-enabled automation to eliminate repetitive manual tasks

**Minimum Qualifications**

- Demonstrated experience working with leading cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- Professional experience in the administration of Linux systems
- Hands-on experience with CI/CD tools like GitHub Workflows, Jenkins, or Gitlab
- Strong understanding of DNS, CDN, SSL, and networking concepts
- Ability to build and deploy containerized software on Cloud
- Hands-on experience with configuration management deployment automation, such as Ansible, Chef, Terraform, Kubernetes, etc.
- Familiar with managing database instances
- Familiar with testing and code quality tools

**Preferred Qualifications**

- Previous history of supporting machine learning workloads
- Prior experience in software development in Java, Python, Node, or related technologies
- Experience with control systems applications (e.g. industrial processes, manufacturing, commercial buildings, SCADA)
- DevOps/SecOps certifications
