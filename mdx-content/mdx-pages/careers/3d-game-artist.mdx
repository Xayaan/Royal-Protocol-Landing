**You are someone who:**

-   Has a passion for game development

-   Has good communication skills who thrives on collaborating with a
    remotely located team

-   Is a motivated, proactive individual who is able to take initiative
    as well as work in a team setting

**You will:**

-   Design Appealing Props

-   Render the Design in 3D, build the prop in 3D, including light
    rigging, hand-painted textures and normal maps

-   Output the model for use in-game

-   Have flexibility and the opportunity to work on a variety of visual
    elements in a game, including characters, props, and environments.

**Basic qualifications:**

-   3+ years game industry employment

-   Knowledge of 2D and 3D game art creation, processes, and development
    pipelines

-   Proficient knowledge of 3D modeling low and high-poly assets and
    texturing

-   Skilled with industry standard tools: 3ds Max/Maya, ZBrush,
    Substance Painter, and Photoshop

-   Strong 2D skills with an ability to hand-paint textures and generate
    concept sketches

-   Experience working in game engines: Unreal Engine, Unity, or
    equivalent

-   A solid understanding of lighting, perspective, and color theory

-   Ability to work independently, while remaining an actively engaged
    member of a small team

**Preferred qualifications:**

-   Traditional illustration/fine arts training

-   Experience rigging, skinning, and animating 3D assets

-   Environment set dressing and lighting skills

-   Prior VFX creation experience

-   Experience in mobile, console, and pc interface platforms

-   Unlimited PTO

-   Health, vision, and dental insurance

-   7% match for purchased team tokens

-   Unlimited Memeing with our team

-   Company owned laptop
