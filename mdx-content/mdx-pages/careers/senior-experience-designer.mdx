**You are someone who**

-   Evaluates designs quickly and creates original high-quality 2D work

-   Has a solid understanding of usability, UX, UI, interaction design,
    mobile development principles, and motion design

-   Understands platform requirements (e.g. Unity)

-   Is able to wireframe or provide visual mockups with a strong focus
    on visual design

-   Uses experience, research, and analysis, to solve UX design problems
    by providing multiple usable options as necessary, including both
    portrait and landscape UI for cross-platform design

-   Adapts creative styles as needed while universally maintaining
    creative, technical, and brand standards

-   Interfaces with key internal client and project stakeholders to
    ensure the user experience reflects customer needs and consistency

-   Possesses an excellent understanding of style guidelines, design
    processes, and creative workflow

-   Skillfully integrates UI components within the Unity game engine

**You Will**

-   Participate in all phases of the design process including conceptual
    design/development, prototypes, user/task flows, art production, and
    game integration

-   Work closely with stakeholders to establish and maintain the visual
    integrity, interaction methods, brand standards, and consistency for
    each game

-   Collaborate with Product Management and Engineering for execution of
    design

**Basic Qualifications**

-   Expert level skills with Adobe Creative Suite
    (Photoshop/Illustrator)

-   Expert working knowledge of Unity, NGUI, motion graphics, and 2d art
    practices

-   Able to cultivate original and creative ideas for products,
    services, or work processes across all Art and Design disciplines

-   Proven ability to take work from concept to in-game

-   Actively seeks to learn and generates new ideas and solutions

**Preferred Qualifications**

-   Proven expertise in new game development (minimum of 5 years related
    work experience)

-   Excellent written and verbal communication for team skills

-   Passion for gaming in general, not just within the mobile space
